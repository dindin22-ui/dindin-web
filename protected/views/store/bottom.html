Welcome to DinDin
Sometimes, you feel like going out. Sometimes, you want to eat dinner in your pajamas while watching your favorite show. Time for takeout! Use DinDin to easily order food from your favorite restaurants. Because life is hectic, but dinner doesn’t have to be.

How does it work? All you have to do is type in your address and DinDin will show you restaurants that are close by. You can even filter by cuisine and places that offer delivery or pick up. Select the restaurant you want and you can see their menu, phone number, hours, address, and website. Add items to your cart, order them, and pick them up when they’re ready. That’s it!
